What you can expect when volunteering for Apache AGE
The role of open-source contributor for Apache AGE is a volunteer role. This means you are not an employee of, or contractor to, Apache AGE and, if you accept the role, you perform all duties on a voluntary basis.

What Apache AGE asks of its volunteers
Project mentors and leaders
Apache AGE values its volunteers and we will endeavour to provide you with:

Full induction, orientation and training relating to the volunteer role
Project leader / supervisor, so that you have the opportunity to ask questions and get feedback about your performance
Opportunity to participate in an Apache Top-Level project as a contributor and become a Committer of that project
Respect for your privacy, including keeping your private information confidential
We ask that you:

Support Apache AGE’s aims and objectives
Understand and comply with the Apache Software Foundation’s and Apache AGE’s contributor policies and procedures
Notify the mentor of any health and safety issues or potentially hazardous situations that may pose a risk to you or others and report any accidents or incidents relating to Apache AGE members including mentors, supervisors and other volunteers
Behave appropriately and courteously to all staff, clients and the public in the course of your role
Let the mentors know immediately if there is anything, or anything arises, that makes you unsuitable or unable to carry out your volunteer role
Be open and honest in your dealings with us and let us know if we can improve our volunteer program and the support that you receive
Mentors and supervisors will be assisting you throughout your volunteering role with Apache AGE.
Project mentors will be dealing with the administrative aspect of your volunteering. Please contact Eya Badal at  eya@apache.org  when you have any questions or concerns about your role as a volunteer, one of the mentors will be contacting you as soon as possible.

Apache AGE / AGE Viewer (Apache Top Level Project)
Project leaders or supervisors, on the other hand, will be managing the performance aspect of your work as a volunteer. They guide, make recommendations to, and review your work on the tasks (or issues) of the Apache AGE and AGE Viewer projects. You can find out the projects available to participate at the Apache AGE and Apache AGE GitHubs and on the Apache AGE website at  https://age.apache.org/ .
You can also consult with the mentors if there is any particular project that you wish not only to participate in but also to have your progress monitored and reviewed by the project leaders and engage in group discussions.
Guide volunteers on how to become an Apache AGE committer or a PMC member
Consult volunteers on becoming a full-time employee of Apache AGE related companies
Help volunteers wishing to take time-off or change from their ongoing project or task
Help resolving any non-technical issue that may arise during volunteering
Monitor volunteer’s involvement with Apache AGE (such as discussion forum activities and mailing lists subscription and posting) and provide related guidance to volunteers
Discuss regularly with project leaders (supervisors) for better supervision of volunteers
Monitor how active volunteers are at the Apache AGE and AGE Viewer GitHub repos
Evaluate volunteers’ works (Pull requests) on the projects available under the Projects menu for Apache AGE and AGE Viewer
Provide technical assistance to volunteers that will support increased contribution
Set goals and deadline of the projects available for Apache AGE and AGE Viewer
Support volunteers’ learning on Apache AGE and AGE Viewer through webinars.
Support volunteers’ engagements and contributions on each project (under the Projects menu) of Apache AGE and AGE Viewer using webinars and mailing-list communications.
