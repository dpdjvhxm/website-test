Join AGE Community!
We are so glad you considering to be a part of the AGE community. Below are some pathways you can follow to help you get started.
These are the recommended paths to progression that will help you reach your objectives.
Ask your AGE-related questions, open various types of discussions, and share your ideas in  GitHub ,   StackOverflow  using
the [apache-age] tag.
A great way to contribute is being a member of the  AGE Project   or  AGE Viewer Project . 
This is a good opportunity to meet other developers and share helpful insights and opinions by working together.
Follow   @apache_age   on Twitter for the most recent news on the project and community!

Mailing Lists
Join the official Apache AGE user and developer mailing lists.
You can get user support, ask general questions and stay up-to-date with project news.

users-subscribe@age.apache.org  is for usage questions, help, and announcements.
dev-subscribe@age.apache.org   is for people who want to contribute code to AGE. 
commits-subscribe@age.apache.org  is for people to follow commits to the project.
